**Fix:** This is expected and documented. Lunar Executor is **not malware** — it has been reviewed by members of the **lunar executor reddit** community. Whitelist the app folder in your security software to resolve this.

### ❌ Script Executes But Has No Effect
**Cause:** Script is outdated or incompatible with the current game version  
**Fix:** Check the **roblox lunar executor discord server** `#script-updates` channel for a current version, or browse the built-in Script Hub for a verified replacement.

### ❌ Android App Crashes on Launch

**Fix:** Go to **Settings → Apps → Lunar → Permissions** and enable all. Confirm device is running Android 8.0 or later. For persistent issues, visit `#android-support` on the **lunar executor discord**.

### ❌ iOS Profile Won't Trust
**Cause:** iOS security blocking unverified developer profiles  
**Fix:** Go to **Settings → General → VPN & Device Management**, find the Lunar profile, and tap **Trust**. If greyed out, restart your device and try again.

### ❌ Lunar Shows "Update Required" After a Roblox Patch
**Cause:** Roblox deployed an update that broke the current injection method  
**Fix:** Click **Update Now** inside the client, or download the latest release from the [releases page](https://lunarwik.github.io/lunar/). Patches are usually available within 12–24 hours of a Roblox deployment.

### ❌ Can't Connect to Lunar Servers
**Cause:** VPN/proxy interference, DNS issues, or scheduled maintenance  
**Fix:** Disable VPN, flush DNS (`ipconfig /flushdns` on Windows), and check the **discord lunar executor** `#server-status` channel for any active outages.
